🤝 Model 2 — Cosine Similarity

Collaborative Filtering

Based on U001's purchase history, find users with similar tastes and recommend books they loved that U001 hasn't read yet.

👤
User U001 — Demo Customer
Purchase history (used to build user-item vector):
How it works: Each user is represented as a vector of book ratings. Cosine similarity measures the angle between vectors — users with similar reading preferences score closer to 1.0. We find the top-5 most similar users, then recommend books they rated highly that U001 hasn't read, weighted by similarity score.

Formula: sim(u,v) = (u·v) / (‖u‖ × ‖v‖)
Recommended for U001 — never read, highly rated by similar users
